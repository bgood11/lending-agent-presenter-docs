## The TL;DR

A demo product that lets a sales rep build a finance quote in seconds, send it to a customer's phone via magic link, capture the customer's acknowledgement of key features asynchronously on their own device, and surface every quote to the retailer's admin portal for audit. No per-rep authentication. No dense wizard. No synchronous tablet handover.

The product is positioned as **tooling for FCA-regulated retailers and brokers**. The retailer (or broker) holds the FCA credit-broking permission. Lending Agent Presenter sells the tooling.

> This is a demo product, built independently. The UI/UX surfaces are real. The backend, magic-link signing, email delivery, PDF generation, and audit storage are documented at engineering-spec depth but not yet implemented in v1. Documentation is for productisation conversations.
